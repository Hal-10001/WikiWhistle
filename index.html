WikiWhistle

Expose injustice, corruption, or abuse—anonymously and safely.

WikiWhistle is a grassroots platform for collecting and amplifying reports of injustice, corruption, mismanagement, and exploitation—locally and globally. Your story matters, whether it’s a massive scandal or a subtle abuse of power. Every submission helps us build a clearer picture of what’s really happening.

FAQ

Why submit? Every story helps. By sharing your experience, you help expose patterns, support others, and put pressure on those who abuse power.

Is it safe? You can remain anonymous. We do not require any personal information unless you want to provide it for follow-up.

What will you do with my submission? Submissions may be used (with your consent) to generate news, inform investigations, and build a database of systemic issues.
